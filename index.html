Sometimes we need to get out of our comfort zone to better ourselves. I challenge you to workout with me 4 days a week for 8 weeks! We will Burn Fat, Increase Endurance, Gain Muscle and to transform ourselves mentally and physically. Are you READY?

8 week crash challenge

GET IN THE BEST SHAPE OF YOUR LIFE WITH MY 8 WEEK CRASH CHALLENGE!

The CHALLENGE is 8 weeks long and has 4 workouts per week that range from 45 to 60 minutes in duration. Each week you’ll get full length videos of the workouts where you'll be able to follow along with me and get that 1 on 1 experience all online!

All the workouts can be done at home! The program is Split into 3 phases.

Weeks 1-3 Is all body weight exercises, calisthenics, static strength & conditioning to build a strong foundation.

Weeks 4-6 We really kick it up a notch and will focus on explosiveness, Strength & endurance. We will Use a sand bag, weighted vest, ab roller and a pair of light dumbbells.

Weeks 6-8 Is our peaking phase and will be a living hell. We will focus a lot on sprint repeats, ballistic movements and most of all conditioning.
